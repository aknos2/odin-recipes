Yakisoba

Description

Here are the ingredients necessary to make the Japanese famous dish Yakisoba.

Ingredients

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Yakisoba seasoning
  • Vegetables of your choice
  • Pork or beef meat

Steps

  1. Boil Yakisoba noodles
  2. Fry meat
  3. Put vegetables inside
  4. Put the noodles inside and pray
