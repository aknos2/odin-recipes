Ramen

Description

Here are the ingredients necessary to make the Japanese famous dish Ramen.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Ramen soup
  • Vegetables of your choice
  • A nice piece of pork meat

Steps

  1. Boil Ramen soup
  2. Put noodles inside
  3. Put vegetables and meat inside
  4. Pray it will be delicious
